The Quran in Flask

Welcome! The Quran in flask intends to provide a tutorial on steps to towards bring the Quranic text through Flask mico framemwork. There are two landing pages:

  • view a particular verse through the link "/chapter no/verse no"
  • view an entire sura by entering the link "/chapter no"

The project is located in the following github repository. Please fork and use the increamental releases to follow the steps towards a full functioning app
https://github.com/abdulbaqi/quranf enjoy!
